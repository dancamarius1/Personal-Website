about Me

History

Since my early childhood i was captivated by the computer world, and this passion never let go of me (and here i have to specially thank my tech teacher). Although i was passionate about tech, I chose to go to college on social specialization, at the moment seemed the right choice. After graduation, i worked in different areas, on positions like technical administrator, party organizer and waiter, among others.

Vision

In my curiosity, i tried to acomplish something by myself, wondering how it would be like to shape this world that i was so fascinated about, the internet. I even remember i had a few attempts in High School to create websites, that's where the spark ignited. Now i'm going this path, doing what it takes to be a web developer.

Goals

I'm currently leveling up my skills in web development, striving to be the best version of myself. Even i didn't worked in this industry until now, i learned from the jobs i had some skills that i apreciate and want to develop them further:
- good comunication
- teamwork and organisation
- responsability attention to details.
With my passion for tech environment, and being a fast learner, i believe i can be a valuable part of a developer team.
